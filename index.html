About the game

It's a fantasy RPG that's all about collecting heroes, smart team building, and character growth

Guild of Guardians gives you that deep RPG experience — you're collecting heroes, putting together teams that actually work well together, and fighting your way through fantasy worlds packed with monsters and treasure. The core gameplay is all about making your team work together, leveling up your characters, exploring dungeons, and finding awesome loot — plus the combat looks pretty intense. We want it to feel epic and polished, like you're playing through a fantasy movie with an amazing story.
